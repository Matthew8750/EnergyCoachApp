## Description

How much energy are you likely to have today—and what should you do with it?

Energy Coach turns selected Apple Health signals and optional daily check-ins into a simple energy estimate, a recovery-risk indicator, and practical wellness suggestions.

CONNECT YOUR HEALTH SIGNALS

With your permission, Energy Coach can read relevant Apple Health information including sleep, resting heart rate, heart-rate variability, activity, exercise, and recovery signals.

UNDERSTAND YOUR DAY

See a clear energy score, the strongest factors affecting it, and suggestions covering recovery, training, sleep, caffeine, and nutrition.

ADD USEFUL CONTEXT

Optional check-ins for mood, stress, symptoms, alcohol, caffeine, and perceived energy help you compare the estimate with how you actually feel.

CHECK YOUR WRIST

The companion Apple Watch app keeps your latest score and primary recommendation close at hand.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

Health information is processed on your devices. Energy Coach does not include advertising or cross-app tracking and does not sell your health data.

Energy Coach provides general wellness information only. It is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition. Seek advice from a qualified healthcare professional when making medical decisions.

## App Review notes

Energy Coach is a general wellness app, not a medical device. It requests read-only Apple Health access after a three-screen explanation and only when the reviewer taps “Update From Health.” The app remains usable with its manual controls if Health access is denied or no Health data is available.

Suggested review flow:

1. Complete the three onboarding screens.
2. Tap “Update From Health.”
3. Choose any Health permissions the reviewer is comfortable granting.
4. Adjust the manual inputs to see the score, explanation, and recommendations update immediately.
5. Save an optional Daily Check-In.

The companion watchOS app receives the latest score through WatchConnectivity. No account or login is required. There is no developer-operated backend, advertising, or cross-app tracking.
