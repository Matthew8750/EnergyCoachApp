Energy Coach

Privacy Policy

Your health data stays under your control. Energy Coach processes wellness information on your devices and does not sell it, use it for advertising, or send it to the developer.

Effective 18 July 2026

Information Energy Coach uses

If you choose to grant access, Energy Coach reads selected Apple Health data such as sleep, heart and recovery signals, activity, body profile information, and nutrition totals. You can also enter lifestyle check-ins such as mood, stress, caffeine, alcohol, symptoms, and perceived energy.

How information is used

This information is used only to calculate your energy estimate, explain relevant signals, personalise wellness suggestions, show trends, and share your latest score with your paired Apple Watch.

Storage and sharing

Energy Coach processes Apple Health information on your device. Check-ins and preferences are stored locally using Apple platform storage. The latest score may be transferred between your own paired iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple’s WatchConnectivity framework.

Energy Coach does not operate a developer server, create an advertising profile, sell personal information, or share health information with advertisers or data brokers.

Apple Health

Health access is optional and controlled through Apple’s permission interface. Energy Coach requests read access only and does not write data to Apple Health. You can review or revoke access at any time in the Health app or iPhone Settings.

Analytics and tracking

Energy Coach does not include third-party advertising, cross-app tracking, or third-party analytics SDKs.

Data retention and deletion

Because information is stored locally, you can remove it by clearing relevant app settings or deleting the app. Apple Health records remain managed by Apple Health and are not deleted when you remove Energy Coach.

Children

Energy Coach is a general wellness tool and is not knowingly designed to collect personal information from children.

Medical disclaimer

Energy Coach provides general wellness estimates and is not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition and is not a substitute for professional medical advice.

Changes

This policy may be updated if the app’s data practices change. The effective date above will be revised when a material update is published.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, open an issue through the Energy Coach support page.
